## Quick Navigation Steps:

### Method 1: Using the Search Bar (Easiest)

1. **Look at the top of your AWS Console**
   - You'll see a search bar that says "Search" (top center)

2. **Type "IAM" in the search bar**
   - Type: `IAM`
   - You'll see suggestions appear

3. **Click on "IAM"** from the suggestions
   - It will say "IAM" with description "Manage access to AWS resources"
   - Click on it

4. **You'll now be on the IAM Dashboard**
   - In the left sidebar, you'll see:
     - Access management
       - Users
       - User groups
       - Roles
       - Policies
     - And more...

5. **Click on "Users"** in the left sidebar
   - This is where you'll create the new user!

---

### Method 2: Using "All services" Menu

1. **Click on "All services"** in the left sidebar (on your Console Home page)

2. **Scroll down or search for "IAM"**
   - Look under "Security, Identity, & Compliance" section
   - Or type "IAM" in the search box

3. **Click on "IAM"**

4. **Click on "Users"** in the left sidebar

---

### Method 3: Direct URL

1. **Copy and paste this URL in your browser:**
   ```
   https://console.aws.amazon.com/iam/home?region=us-east-1#/users
   ```

2. **This will take you directly to the Users page**

---
